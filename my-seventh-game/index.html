The enemies will spawn on the edges of the screen and make their way towards the player and shoot continuously as they progess. If the player is hit by a laser or enemy the player dies and must start over. The goal is for the player to accrue as many points as they can by shooting enemies.
  • Strategies: The player will need to keep careful stock of both the enemy lasers and enemies themselves. Prioritsing targets will be the best way of surviving. Shoot enemies to decrease the amount of lasers being fired.
