  • Gameplay: The player can move up, down left, and right by pressing the W, A, S, and D keys respectively. They can also shoot lasers to kill enemies by pressing the spacebar. The player will need to move to both avoid enemies and lasers, and line up shots to kill them. The enemies will spawn at the top of the scene and make their way towards the player once the player is within a certain distance or shoot continuously as they progess down the screen. If the player is hit by a laser or enemy the player dies and must start over. The goal is for the player to accrue as many points as they can by shooting enemies. Stroy wise the player is fighting off COVID-19 cells and trying to prevent becoming infected.
